Detailed Description

Responsible for periodically spawning enemy units around the map.

This class spawns enemies at random positions on a circle around the spawner. Enemies appear either on the left or right side relative to the center and move toward the specified target carriage.

void Spawner.SpawnEnemy ( )
inlineprivate

Spawns a new enemy at a random position around the spawner.

The enemy is spawned on a circular radius around the spawner, either to the left or right side, with a small random angle offset. After spawning, the enemy receives a reference to the target carriage.

Chooses a base angle: left (270°) or right (90°).

Adds a random variation to the base angle.

Calculates the spawn position on the circular radius.

Instantiates the enemy and assigns its target.
