\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Controls the movement and lifecycle of a ground row segment. 

The ground row moves backward continuously to simulate map movement. After travelling a certain distance, it triggers the creation of a new ground row and eventually destroys itself once it exceeds its lifetime distance.
